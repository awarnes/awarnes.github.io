About Awarnes

I have been programming since I was 12, hacking away at things like Python, the TI-83, and various basic drag-and-drop programming languages. However, the drives of life took over, and programming of most any kind fell by the wayside.

Until, the end of 2016 that is.

By that point, the allure of the financial services industry had worn off and it was time for something different. Once again the world of programming beckoned....

I quit my job, enrolled in the Full Stack Python Developer Bootcamp with PDX Code Guild, and haven't looked back since.

Having recently graduated from the bootcamp I am moving on to the class in React and beginning to look for work as a developer.

The future is still wide open with plenty of excitement to come!
